# General Reviewer Skill

## When to Use
- Content type is unclear or mixed
- General critical analysis needed
- Fallback when no specific skill matches

## Workflow

1. **Content Classification**
   - Identify content type and purpose
   - Determine appropriate review criteria

2. **Critical Analysis**
   - Factual accuracy
   - Internal consistency
   - Completeness
   - Clarity and communication

3. **Risk Assessment**
   - What could go wrong?
   - What assumptions might fail?
   - What's missing?
